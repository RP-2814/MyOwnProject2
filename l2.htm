Sample 6 Days / 5 Nights Bali Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival & Uluwatu Sunset
Arrive at the airport and be transferred to your hotel.
Head to Uluwatu Temple to enjoy the sunset and witness a traditional Kecak Fire Dance.
Conclude the day with a fresh seafood dinner on Jimbaran Beach.
Day 2: Kintamani Volcano & Ubud Cultural Tour Explore the picturesque Kintamani region with its volcano views.
Discover the cultural heart of Ubud, visiting sites like the Sacred Monkey Forest and Tegalalang Rice Terrace.
Day 3: Bedugul Temples & Tanah Lot Sunset Visit the serene Ulun Danu Beratan Temple in Bedugul.
Head to Tanah Lot Temple to experience its iconic sunset.
Day 4: East Bali Exploration Explore the Lempuyang Temple, known as the "Gate of Heaven," for breathtaking views.
You might also visit Tirta Ganga Water Palace or explore Tenganan Traditional Village.
Day 5: Island Adventure to Nusa Penida Take a day trip to Nusa Penida to see beautiful beaches like Kelingking and Angel's Billabong, and enjoy snorkeling.
Day 6: Relaxation & Departure Enjoy some free time for last-minute souvenir shopping or relaxation at the hotel.
After checking out, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure.
Tips for a 6-Day Bali Trip Consider island hopping: A day trip to Nusa Penida offers stunning coastal scenery and adventure.
Cultural immersion: Don't miss the chance to see a traditional Balinese dance performance and visit ancient temples.
Book a tour package: A package can streamline planning and include key attractions, activities, and transfers.
Flexibility: Many tour packages offer flexible itineraries, allowing for customization to suit your interests.
