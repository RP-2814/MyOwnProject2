Typical Itinerary & Activities

Day 1: Arrival & Dubai Marina Arrive in Dubai, check into your hotel, and enjoy an evening dhow cruise on the Dubai Marina, often including dinner and entertainment.
Day 2: Dubai City Tour Explore the city's landmarks and modern structures with a guided city tour.
Day 3: Desert Safari Experience a desert safari, a common package highlight, for thrilling activities and a unique cultural experience.
Day 4: Departure Enjoy your final morning in Dubai before heading to the airport for your departure.
What's Usually Included:
Accommodation: Stays in hotels for the three nights.
Transfers: Transportation to and from the airport and between locations.
Tours: Guided city tours and desert safari experiences.
Dhow Cruise: A traditional dhow cruise, often on Dubai Marina.

Tips for Planning

Best Time to Visit: The most pleasant time to visit Dubai is from November to March when temperatures are mild (15°C to 25°C), making it ideal for outdoor activities.
Budget: Consider the best time to visit for your budget, as some seasons are more expensive than others.
Booking: Book with a reputable travel provider to ensure you get a good itinerary and price, according to Tripadvisor and other sources.
