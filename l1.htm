Inclusions in a 5-day / 4-night Paris package

Accommodation and transportation:

Hotel accommodation:
Four nights in a hotel, with your choice of class (e.g., 3-star, 4-star) determining the average price.
Meals:
Daily breakfast at the hotel is often included.
Airport transfers:
Travel between the Paris International Airport and your hotel is generally arranged by the tour provider.
Transportation:
Travel between sights is typically via a tour bus or on a hop-on-hop-off service.
Most packages include visits to major attractions such as:
Eiffel Tower Louvre Museum Arc de Triomphe and Champs-Élysées Notre Dame Cathedral Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur
Seine River cruise: An evening cruise along the Seine River is often part of the itinerary, sometimes with an added dinner.
Evening entertainment:
Some packages feature an evening cabaret show, like the famous Lido Show or Moulin Rouge.
Day trips: Many itineraries set aside a day for an excursion outside of the city, with popular options being: Palace of Versailles Disneyland Paris
Customizable activities:
You may have free time to explore and book your own activities, such as a cooking class or a food tour.
Exclusions:
It's important to note that lunch, dinner (unless specified), entrance fees, travel insurance, and personal expenses are usually not included in the base package price.
